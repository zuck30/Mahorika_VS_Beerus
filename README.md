<h2>Gameplay Overview</h2>

### Beerus (God of Destruction)
- **Divine Energy** ‚Äî his remaining power / "health"
- **Hakai** ‚Äî erasure attack (initially devastating, weakens as adaptation grows)
- Each Hakai usage forces Mahoraga's wheel to turn faster

### Mahoraga & Rika
- **HP** ‚Äî Mahoraga's physical form integrity
- **Adaptation %** ‚Äî 0 ‚Üí 100% (100% = complete immunity to Hakai)
- **Sustain Flow** ‚Äî Rika channels cursed energy to heal / keep Mahoraga alive
- **Accelerate Adaptation** ‚Äî Focus mode: faster progress, but much more vulnerable
